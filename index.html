AN EMPOWERING CODING SCHOOL

We are  a Socially Driven coding school in Haiti, that intends to empower disadvantaged communities by bringing education and employment oppotunities to the youth in inner cities who can later use that knowledge to "Code" a better Haiti. 

Solution for Entrepreneurs

Tech entrepreneurs need faster and more cost effective way to recruit talents for their companies. By partnering with us, they will have access to a pool of extremely smart individuals who went through an intensive program, gathering practical expertise that will be valuable to their companies.

Solution for Students

Greatness and inteliggence are equally distrubuted around the world, but opportunities are not. This is why we want to provide an entirely FREE EDUCATION to the brightest students in inner cities of Haiti. We will then ensure employment opportunities for each one of those students in growing startups around the world.

Problems We Solve

We pride ourselves that our business model is socially driven, and that we are working towards building a more equal world. Our mission is also deeply rooted in a passion for helping our native country Haiti. Our business model connects the dots to create a win-win situation across the board. Companies around the world need global talents, Haitian youth is looking for education and employment opportunities, and the Haitian economy is desperately in need of an increase of GDP via production and export (in this case, export of our knowledge and services). We believe that we can propel growth in the technology sector in Haiti to create jobs, and faciliate technology to become an impactul factor of economic development

Education Program

Our Education process ensures that each student get a chance to mature intelectually as a software engineer and as a person. 

Recruitement Process

-Online IQ Exam 
-Essays on how applicants will use knowledge provided by PIT to solve problems in his/her community
-2 weeks assessment bootcamp

1st Year of Intensive Education

-A full year of an intensive curriculum in programming languages such as: Ruby / Ruby on Rails, Python / Django, PHP / Laravel, MEAN Stack, & Mobile Development (Android and iOS).
-English class
-Leadership Skills classes

2nd Year Internship / Education

-Intensive curriculum based on practical expertise
-Internship with us, selling software development services for individual clients
-English class

3rd and 4th year of Emloyment

-Formal employment opportunities at Tech company
 -Business and Management Skills classes 
-English courses

Incubator Opportunities

-Business competition, electing student's ideas that have the most potential, to be integrated in our incubator, which is how we intend to have real impact on the Haitian economy, facilitating the creation of more local tech companies

Our Story

Despite being awarded several scholarships that could cover his cost of Education, Esdras Malbranche always felt a sense of guilt, as most of his former classmates in Haiti were not as lucky as him. Those classmates did not have a chance to pursue secondary education, and Esdras' Mother, as a Haitian educator for more than 30 years, knows too well how this scenario is played for every promotion of students she graduates. This is why they've cofounded PIT
